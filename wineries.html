Lake Chelan's Finest Wineries

The Lake Chelan AVA is one of Washington State's most exciting wine regions — a hidden gem tucked between the Cascade Mountains and the Columbia Basin. With over 30 wineries ranging from boutique hillside estates to full-service lakefront destinations, the Chelan Valley offers an unmatched wine tasting experience.

Siren Song Winery

A crown jewel of the Chelan wine scene, Siren Song crafts European-inspired wines on a dramatic lakefront estate. Their Rhône-style varietals — Syrah, Grenache, and Viognier — reflect the unique terroir of Washington's Lake Chelan AVA. The tasting room opens onto a sunlit terrace with sweeping lake views, frequent live music, and a warm, sophisticated atmosphere that keeps guests coming back season after season.

Lake Chelan Winery

As one of the original pioneers of the Lake Chelan AVA, Lake Chelan Winery has been crafting estate wines since 2000. Their fruit is grown on-site in the sun-drenched Chelan Valley, producing exceptional Pinot Gris, Gewürztraminer, and other varietals that showcase the region's unique microclimate. The beloved lakeside BBQ deck and relaxed vineyard atmosphere make every visit feel like a true getaway from the everyday.

Plan Your Lake Chelan Wine Tour

The Lake Chelan American Viticultural Area (AVA) spans over 24,000 acres across the Chelan Valley, producing a diverse range of wines from bold Cabernets to delicate Rieslings. The region's unique combination of high elevation, long sunny days, and cool nights creates ideal growing conditions that rival any wine region in the Pacific Northwest.
